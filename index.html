Portfolio Findings

"Spare Parts Growth from 2019 to 2025 is primarily a project of strategic price increases rather than growth in the quantity of parts sold."

While revenue has nearly tripled, unit volume has only expanded by 43%. This decoupling proves that pricing power, rather than logistics throughput, is the primary driver of total value.

Nuclear Performance

"This trend is mirrored in the Nuclear segment, where massive revenue scaling was achieved through pricing resilience, outperforming unit growth by a factor of 5:1."

Nuclear revenue reached a 320.9 index, while its unit index remained at 145. This confirms that the nuclear business is growing by capturing significantly more value per shipment.
